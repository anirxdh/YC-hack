Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to SMS messages sent by Anirudh's personal assistant service. This is a personal, non-commercial messaging service used for sending notifications and reminders to consenting individuals.

Information We Collect

We only collect phone numbers that are voluntarily provided to us by the recipient for the purpose of sending text messages.

How We Use Information

Phone numbers are used solely to send SMS messages as requested. We do not sell, share, or distribute phone numbers to any third parties.

Data Retention

Phone numbers are retained only as long as needed to send messages and are deleted upon request.

Opt-Out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Once opted out, no further messages will be sent.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reply to any message or contact the sender directly.
